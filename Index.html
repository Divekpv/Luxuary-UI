Message from the Founder

From boardrooms to bespoke skincare, my journey as a successful businesswoman has been one of embracing transformation and empowering others to do the same.

With a proven track record of building thriving businesses, I recognized a glaring gap in Kerala's beauty landscape: the lack of a truly world-class skincare destination. This realization ignited a new passion within me – to create a sanctuary where science-backed solutions would meet personalized care, where every individual could embark on a journey towards their most radiant self.

And so, Luxuria was born. At Luxuria, we've assembled a team of highly skilled medical practitioners, each renowned in their field. Together, we share a commitment to delivering the highest standards of care, utilizing state-of-the-art, FDA-approved technology previously unavailable in the region”.


Dr. Fathima Fabin
